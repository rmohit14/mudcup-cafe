Our Story

A Cozy Corner Worth Every Visit

Open Now · Closes 10:30 PM

MudCups is more than a café — it's an experience. Nestled near VLB College with a beautiful rooftop, warm hanging lamps, and lush greenery, every corner is designed to make you stay longer. We serve love in every cup and plate.
